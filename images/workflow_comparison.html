Why Homography-Based Approach?

Our homography-based implementation strikes the optimal balance between accuracy and computational efficiency. By leveraging a 4-point calibration matrix with bottom-center anchoring, we achieve perspective-corrected measurements in real-world units (m/s) while maintaining real-time performance. This approach provides both speed magnitude and direction estimation, making it ideal for traffic monitoring and surveillance applications where understanding object trajectory is as important as speed measurement.
